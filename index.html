By focusing on "Attribution First." I audit your CRM integration and GA4 signals to ensure Google's AI is bidding on high-quality sales leads, not just junk form fills. This reduces wasted ad spend by up to 30% and improves lead quality significantly.

Why is Google Analytics 4 (GA4) better for tracking?

GA4 uses an event-based model that tracks the modern user journey across devices and platforms. Combined with server-side tracking, it provides more accurate data in the post-iOS 14 era, helping you make better optimization decisions.
